About Me

Hi, My name is David Wylie. I grew up in a military family and had the opportunity to see some of the world. It also started my interest in travel and food. I like cooking as much as I like eating food. I went to college for hospitaliy and worked in food service for about 21 years.

As much as I liked working in restaurants. I wanted to switch to something with more consistency in my regular day to day life. I've alway had an interest in technology, and thought I would try to pursue a career in it. This brought me to U Penn Coding Bootcamp which will help to propel me into the next Chapter of my Life.

I'm hoping that some of my skills from the restaurant industry will be able to transfer over to the tech world. I have been working/managing teams for the last 15 years. I know how integral teams are in the tech world. With my role as manager, I have had to hone my communication skills by all my interaction with staff and guest.
